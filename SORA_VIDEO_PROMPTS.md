## Tips for Using These Prompts with Sora:

1. **Be Specific**: Add details about camera angles, lighting, mood, and style
2. **Length**: Specify if you want short clips (5-10 seconds) or longer videos (30-60 seconds)
3. **Style**: Mention if you want realistic, animated, cinematic, or social media style
4. **Iterate**: Try variations of prompts to get different results
5. **Combine**: You can combine elements from multiple prompts
6. **Brand Colors**: Mention if you want specific colors (blue and black for Parkly)
7. **Music Note**: Add a note about what type of music would work (though Sora generates video, not audio)

## Customization Variables:

For any prompt, you can customize:
- **Location**: Replace "Levi's Stadium" with any venue
- **Time of Day**: Specify morning, afternoon, golden hour, nighttime
- **Weather**: Sunny, cloudy, rainy
- **Camera Style**: Aerial, close-up, wide shot, tracking shot
- **Mood**: Energetic, calm, professional, friendly
- **Duration**: Short (5-10s), medium (15-30s), long (30-60s)
