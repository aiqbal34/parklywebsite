## Tips for Email Pitches:
1. **Personalize:** Always customize the greeting and reference something specific about the recipient
2. **Keep it concise:** Aim for 150-200 words max
3. **Clear CTA:** Make it obvious what you want them to do next
4. **Follow up:** Send a follow-up email 3-5 days later if no response
5. **Test subject lines:** Try different subject lines to see what gets opens
6. **Proofread:** Always check for typos and grammar
